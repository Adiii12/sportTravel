Welcome to Sports Friendly Hotels

Welcome here

Welcome to Sports Friendly Hotels - your prime destination for active and sustainable travel experiences. Founded in 2023, our platform springs from a deep-rooted passion for sports, adventure, and the vision that travel should have a enriching and positive impact on both individuals and our planet.

Our Mission

Here is our mission

At Sports Friendly Hotels, our mission is to revolutionize the way people travel. We believe in a travel culture where discovery goes hand in hand with activity, sustainability, and a deep respect for nature and cultural diversity. We strive to empower you to pursue your passion for movement anywhere in the world while also making a positive contribution to local communities and the environment at every step of your journey.

Our Vision

Here is our Vission

Our aim is to be recognized worldwide as the leading platform for active and conscious travelers. Through seamless collaboration with sports-friendly hotels and local partners, we are committed to providing exceptional, tailor-made travel experiences that exceed your expectations.

Our Promise

Here is our promise

We are committed to not only offering an exceptional travel experience but also the best value. That's why we guarantee that you will always get the lowest price when you book through Sports Friendly Hotels. If you find a lower price elsewhere, the hotel will adjust the price to ensure that you always get the best offer. Moreover, we reward every booking with an additional benefit: a cashback of 2% on the total cost, expressing our appreciation for choosing Sports Friendly Hotels and providing even more value for your active travels.

Our Values

Here is our values
  • Authenticity: "We celebrate the unique stories and preferences of every traveler, with personalized experiences that resonate with your individual passions."
  • Collaboration: "We cherish our community of travelers and partners, working towards a common goal to change the travel industry for the better."
  • Trust: "Openness and honesty are paramount to us, with transparent communication about our sustainability efforts and the benefits we offer."
  • Perseverance: "We are dedicated to continuous improvement, overcoming challenges to create a more sustainable travel future."
  • Curiosity: "We encourage a spirit of discovery, with openness to new cultures, experiences, and learning from every journey."
