Reason

With calamities and insolvency insurance, you get twofold security: protection against unforeseen, serious events that may affect your trip, and financial security in case a travel provider is unable to deliver booked services due to insolvency. This insurance is optional, but it can offer valuable peace of mind by significantly reducing the risk of unforeseen costs and issues during your trip.

Insurance Details
Calamity Insurance

Compensation for unenjoyed travel days, additional accommodation costs, or repatriation assistance.

Insolvency Insurance

Compensation for prepaid, undelivered services, and assistance in finding alternative travel arrangements.

Risks
